🌱 ProofHarvest

Trust You Can Track: Escrow Built for Real Farmers and Ethical Buyers

The Problem

Small-scale farmers often face income losses due to delayed payments, broken agreements, and the absence of verifiable records in supply chains. Without transparent systems, both buyers and suppliers operate on hope rather than trust—leading to disputes, cash flow problems, and broken relationships that hurt everyone involved.

Our Solution

ProofHarvest: A milestone-based smart escrow system that creates trust through transparency:

  • Locks payments securely until predefined conditions are met
  • Releases funds automatically as each milestone is verified
  • Creates transparent, immutable records that prove ethical transactions happened

How It Works

1 Create Agreement
Define custom milestones with specific verification requirements
2 Secure Funds
Buyer's payment is locked in blockchain escrow (USDC)
3 Verify Progress
Each milestone requires documented proof of completion
4 Release Payment
Funds transfer directly to supplier upon verification

What You Get

  • Buyers get: Risk protection, delivery verification, and proof of ethical sourcing practices
  • Suppliers get: Guaranteed payment, improved cash flow, and peace of mind that agreements will be honored
  • Everyone gets: Shared records, trust without spreadsheets, and fewer headaches dealing with disputes
